Durable and Reliable Bathroom Sink Drain Stopper

Crafted from high-quality brass and 304 stainless steel, this drain stopper is built to last. Its robust construction ensures exceptional resistance to corrosion and wear, making it a long-term solution for your bathroom needs.

Designed without overflow, this drain stopper is perfectly suited for vessel and undermount sinks. It provides a seamless fit and guarantees efficient drainage, achieving a water flow rate of up to 27L/min. This makes it ideal for maintaining a clean and functional bathroom environment without the risk of water pooling.

Efficient Water Management for Modern Bathrooms

With a drain hole diameter range of 1.53 to 1.88 inches and compatibility with most standard sink thicknesses up to 1.97 inches, this drain stopper is versatile and easy to install. It comes with a detailed guide to ensure a smooth setup process, even for those new to DIY projects.

Being CUPC certified, it meets rigorous standards for efficiency and safety, making it a top choice for homeowners and bathroom enthusiasts alike.
