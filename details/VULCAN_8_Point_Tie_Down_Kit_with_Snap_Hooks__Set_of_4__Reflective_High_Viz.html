VULCAN 8-Point Tie Down Kit

This professional-grade vehicle tie down kit is engineered to provide unmatched reliability and safety during transport. Trusted by trucking professionals and auto haulers worldwide, it's designed to secure vehicles with precision and confidence. With its reflective high-viz webbing, it ensures visibility in all light conditions, whether it's daylight or nightfall.

Constructed from durable, extra thick polyester, the 8-point tie down system boasts a breaking strength of up to 14,000 lbs, making it suitable for heavy-duty use. The set includes four high-quality straps, along with snap hooks and ratchets that make setup and attachment quick and easy. These components are designed to work seamlessly with D rings on your trailer or vehicle deck, eliminating the need for metal contact and protecting your equipment from scratches and damage.

Perfect for transporting cars, SUVs, and light trucks, this kit is ideal for use on flatbed or roll-back wrecker vehicles. The self-adjusting design allows for fast securing around tires, and the ergonomic handles make operation comfortable and safe, even when handling with gloves. Built to meet CVSA and DOT standards, it delivers peace of mind with every trip.

  • Professional-grade tie down system for secure vehicle transport
  • Reflective high-viz webbing for maximum visibility in all light conditions
  • Durable 14,000 lb breaking strength polyester webbing
  • Ergonomic ratchets with molded handles for easy and safe operation
  • Self-adjusting cinch up design for quick tire securing
