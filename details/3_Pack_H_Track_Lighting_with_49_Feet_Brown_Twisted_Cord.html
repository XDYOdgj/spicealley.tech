3-Pack H Track Pendant Light with Vintage Design

Add a touch of vintage charm to your space with this 3-pack H track lighting fixture. The twist cable and ceramic base create a unique blend of retro and modern styling, making it a versatile lighting solution for any room. The compact, elegant design is perfect for small spaces and complements ambient lighting with a soft, warm glow.

The pendant lights come with a 4.9 feet brown twisted cord that allows easy installation and adjustable placement. Whether you're decorating an entryway, a loft, a dorm room, or a corridor, these fixtures bring a refined look without compromising functionality.

Installation is simple and requires no professional help. Simply turn off the power, place the fixture in the direction of the arrow, and rotate it 90 degrees. These lights are ideal for dark areas, enhancing the mood and style of your interior.

Each light offers a customizable hook or adapter option, making them adaptable to different ceiling types and lighting setups. They are a great choice for modern, minimalist, and retro decor styles, adding a statement piece to your living room or dining area with minimal effort.
