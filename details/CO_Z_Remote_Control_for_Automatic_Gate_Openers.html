Effortless Control for Your Automatic Gates

The CO-Z Remote Control is a reliable and user-friendly solution for managing your automatic gate opener. With a 100-foot range, it gives you the freedom to open your gate from a distance, keeping your hands free and your daily routine smooth. The built-in auto-close feature lets you customize the gate closing time to 12, 24, or 36 seconds, enhancing both security and convenience. Designed with ease of use in mind, the ANT button makes programming a breeze, ensuring compatibility with most existing gate systems without complicated setups.

Crafted from high-quality aluminum alloy, this remote is both lightweight and robust, providing durability that withstands daily usage and harsh weather conditions. Whether you're handling a sliding gate, driveway gate, or any standard 433.92 MHz non-encrypted gate opener, the CO-Z Remote Control offers seamless integration and long-lasting performance. It's the perfect gate opener accessory for both residential and commercial properties, delivering peace of mind and hassle-free access whenever you need it.
