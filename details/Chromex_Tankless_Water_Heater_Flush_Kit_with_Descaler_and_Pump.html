Chromex Tankless Water Heater Flush Kit

Effortless maintenance for your tankless water heater with everything you need in one convenient package.

Why Choose This Kit?

This complete 6-piece flush kit is engineered to keep your tankless water heater performing at its best. Whether you're a homeowner or a professional, it's the perfect solution for removing mineral buildup and restoring efficiency.

With a 5-gallon bucket with lid, a powerful 1/6 HP pump, and durable PVC coated hoses, the process is both quick and easy. All you need is 30 minutes to complete the cleaning, making it a time-effective investment for long-term use.

Key Features

  • 6-Piece Comprehensive Kit: Everything you need to clean your tankless water heater in one place.
  • Citric Acid Based Descaler: Safe for metals, PVC, rubber, and ABS plastic components.
  • 1/6 HP Extra Strength Pump: Ensures efficient flow and fast cleaning results.
  • PVC Coated Hoses: Designed to resist corrosion and prevent leaks during use.
  • Elevated Pump Grate: Allows easy access and full visibility for effective maintenance.

Enjoy Peace of Mind

Our non-corrosive, solvent-free descaling solution is ideal for potable water systems, ensuring safety and reliability. The kit is compatible with major brands like Rinnai, Bosch, Noritz, and Rheem, making it a versatile tool for any setting.

Backed by a 1-year warranty and simple return policy, you can trust the quality and performance of this kit. Keep your water heater running smoothly for years with this essential plumbing maintenance tool.
